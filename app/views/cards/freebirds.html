Free Birds In theaters: November 1st, 2013

JULY 1ST, 2013 JOHNATHAN DOE

In this hilarious, adventurous buddy comedy for audiences of all ages, directed by Jimmy Hayward (Horton Hears a Who!), two turkeys from differences and team up to travel back in time to change the course of history - and get turkey off the Thanksgiving menu for good.

Cast: Owen Wilson, Woody Harrelson, Amy Poehler, Dan Fogler, Lesley Nicol, George Takei.
